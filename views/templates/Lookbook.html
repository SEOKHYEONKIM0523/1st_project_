Fall / Winter 2023

In our FW23 collection we continue our exploration of fabrics through texture
and color stories. Earth tones, off-whites, brushing processes, and knits
beget a tour de force of autumnal shirting and knitwear. A new family of
Brushed Fall Oxfords set the pace of the collection with hopsacks,
herringbone tweeds, and Jumbo Corduroy following suit. Vintage prints and
retro-inspired Triple Yarns embrace our affection for Americana, while
intricately constructed patchwork fabrics nod to our history of
manufacturing. Accompanying our shirts is an array of Alpaca wool sweaters
and a capsule of Brushed Oxford neckwear.
